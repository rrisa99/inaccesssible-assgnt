AWESOME FOOTBALL

WHY FOOTBALL IS THE BEST SPORT IN THE WORLD

BY ORISA EKPETTE

There are those who argue over which sport is the most popular in the world. Most people would argue that the most popular sports are boxing, rugby, cricket, or basketball. However, in my opinion football is the best and most interesting sport in the world.

MY REASONS

  1. The Subtle Greatness Of Real Madrid
  2. The Continental Competition e.g the World Cup brings the World together
  3. The World Cup is also the Most Popular Tournament in the World
  4. The Best Athlete in the World Plays in the Sport
  5. Transfer Saga and World Records
